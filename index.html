WHAT IS NEKORAPA?

Nekopara (Japanese: ネコぱら) is a series of adult visual novels developed by NEKO WORKs and published by Sekai Project. The first game in the series, Nekopara Vol. 1, was released in December 2014.[1] The series takes place in a world where catgirls and humans live alongside each other, and the series is about "life with adorable catgirls"
